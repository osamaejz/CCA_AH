How CCA Works

Computer based assessment done by user

The evaluation consists of a series of cognitive and mental health tests. The user can take this assessment on their own without any prior training.

Analysis of responses and computation of scores

The inputs are processed, the tests are scored, and the results are compared to healthy standards using smart cloud-based software. The developed algorithms then evaluate the findings and map them onto cognitive domains to produce a competency matrix.

ABOUT US

Welcome to CCA - Corporate Cognitive Assessment

A digital cognitive assessment tool, developed by Neurocomputation Laboratory, National Center of Artificial Intelligence, Pakistan, that offers cognitive profiling, along with the identification of brain patterns activated during cognitive tasks in order to better discern intellectual capabilities, and provide customized action plan empowering the individuals to deliver their full potential.
